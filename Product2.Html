5 seater Sofa

A 5-seater sofa is a spacious seating arrangement designed to comfortably accommodate five people. It is ideal for large families or living rooms where more seating is required. These sofas are available in various designs such as sectional, modular, or straight styles to match different interiors.

key Points:
Seating for five people Spacious and comfortable Suitable for large rooms Available in different styles

Wingback Sofa

A wingback sofa is a classic style of sofa featuring a high backrest with side “wings” that extend down from the top. Originally designed for comfort and support, it now represents elegance and luxury in modern interiors. These sofas are commonly used in living rooms, hotels, and lounges..

key Points:
High back with side wings Elegant and classic design Comfortable neck & back support Often tufted (button design) Wooden legs or frame

Loveseat Sofa

A loveseat sofa is a small sofa designed to seat two people comfortably. It is compact in size, making it perfect for small living spaces, bedrooms, or offices.Loveseats are stylish and cozy, often used for intimate seating or as an additional piece alongside larger sofas.

key Points:
Designed for 2 people Compact and space-saving Comfortable and cozy seating Available in many styles (modern, classic, fabric, etc.) Ideal for small rooms or apartments
