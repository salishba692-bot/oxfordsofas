Chesterfield Sofa

A Chesterfield sofa is a timeless classic known for its iconic tufted design, rolled arms, and deep button detailing. Originally crafted in the 18th century, it has become a symbol of sophistication and luxury. This sofa adds a grand, elegant touch to any living space, study, or lounge area.

Key points:
Deep button tufting on back and arms
Equal height arms and back
Available in leather and velvet
Timeless and elegant design
Ideal for formal living rooms and studies

Recliner Sofa

A recliner sofa is designed for ultimate relaxation, featuring adjustable backrest and footrest positions. It allows users to sit upright or recline fully, making it perfect for watching TV, reading, or resting. Available in manual and electric operation, recliner sofas are a top choice for modern comfort-focused homes.

Key Points:
Adjustable backrest and footrest
Manual or electric mechanism
Padded armrests for extra comfort
Available in leather and fabric
Perfect for home theaters and lounges

Sleeper Sofa

A sleeper sofa, also known as a sofa bed, is a multi-functional piece of furniture that converts from a sofa into a comfortable bed. It is an excellent space-saving solution for small apartments, guest rooms, or studio flats. The hidden mattress folds out easily for overnight guests, making it both practical and stylish.

Key Points:
Converts into a full or queen-size bed
Built-in foldable mattress
Saves space efficiently
Ideal for guests and studio apartments
Available in various fabric options

Loveseat Sofa

A loveseat sofa is a compact two-seater sofa designed for couples or small spaces. It offers the same comfort and style as a full-size sofa but in a smaller footprint. Perfect for bedrooms, small living rooms, or cozy reading corners, a loveseat adds charm and intimacy to any space.

Key Points:
Compact two-seater design
Perfect for small rooms and bedrooms
Cozy and intimate seating
Available in fabric, leather, and velvet
Easy to move and rearrange
